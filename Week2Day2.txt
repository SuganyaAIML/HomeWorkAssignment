. Document Overview
Project Name: Retail Telecom – SIM Card & Phone Sales
Document Type: Test Strategy
Testing Type: Functional & End-to-End Testing
Methodology: Scrum
2. Scope of Testing
In Scope
In-store POS systems (Windows/Linux with peripherals like barcode scanners, receipt printers)
E-commerce website (Chrome, Safari, Edge, Firefox)
Mobile applications (Android & iOS – QAT environment)
Out of Scope
Third-party systems internal logic (only integration validation covered)
3. Business Processes Covered
The following end-to-end user journeys will be validated:
New SIM purchase (Prepaid/Postpaid)
Phone purchase (standalone / bundled with SIM)
SIM activation & number provisioning
KYC / identity verification
Plan selection, recharge & billing
Order placement & payment processing
Returns, cancellations, and exchanges
